StaySync

A vacation rental platform named StaySync. It showcases a variety of accommodations worldwide, including beachfront cottages, mountain retreats, urban lofts, and unique stays like treehouses and castles. Each listing provides a brief description, nightly rate (with an additional 18% GST), and a "View Details" option for more information.​ The platform offers a user-friendly interface with categories such as Hotels, Mountains, Castles, Pools, Camping, Beach, and Snow. Users can explore listings, sign up, log in, and even list their own properties.
